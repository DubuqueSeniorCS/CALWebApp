CREATIVE ADVENTURE LAB

ABOUT US

As an Innovation Campus, Creative Adventure Lab envisions a community brimming with creative capacity; one that’s full of creative and innovative thinkers. To achieve this vision, Creative Adventure Lab helps people of all ages discover, strengthen, and apply creative thinking and innovation skills. That’s why we fuse science, art, and technology into hands-on adventures that foster creative thinking, drives innovation, leads to social and economic growth, and improves the quality of life.
